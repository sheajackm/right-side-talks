Who We Are

The Voice of Young
Conservative America

Right Side Talks LLC is a national youth conservative media organization built by young Americans, for young Americans. We believe the next generation deserves a platform that reflects their values—freedom, faith, family, and country.

We create bold, unapologetic content that cuts through the noise of mainstream media and delivers the perspective that too often goes unheard on campuses and in communities across the country.

Whether you're a first-time voter or a lifelong patriot, Right Side Talks is your home for authentic conservative media made by people just like you.

Join Our Movement

Freedom of Speech

We champion the First Amendment and believe every American has the right to speak their truth without fear of censorship or cancel culture.

America First

We stand proudly for American exceptionalism, our Constitution, and the principles that have made the United States the greatest nation on Earth.

Youth-Driven

Our content is created by young conservatives who understand the challenges and opportunities facing their generation firsthand.

National Reach

From coast to coast, we connect conservative young Americans and amplify their voices in the national conversation.

The People Behind the Mic

Meet the Team

The voices, writers, and producers driving the Right Side Talks movement from coast to coast.

Jack Shea
Owner & Founder

Jack is a natural-born leader and entrepreneur from Jamestown, NY. A Business Management major at St. Bonaventure University, he serves as President of the SBU College Republicans chapter and Executive Director of the New York State College Republicans, bringing bold conservative leadership to the next generation.

Nick Cribbet
Vice President of Operations

Nick is a St. Bonaventure University student with strong aspirations of becoming an aerospace engineer. Relatively new to the political space, Nick brings fresh energy and a commitment to building connections across the aisle, focused on moving this nation away from corruption and toward a better future for all Americans.

Cecelia Brown
Creative Director & Executive Producer

Cecelia is an undergraduate student at St. Bonaventure University from Upstate New York, on track to graduate in May 2027 with a degree in Psychology and a minor in Political Science. Always deeply engaged in the political space, Cecelia is driven by one core belief: doing what is in the best interest of American citizens.

Caleb Grubb
Director of Talent Acquisition

Caleb is a young Christian conservative from Upstate New York with a passion for bringing restoration to the American political system. A grassroots activist, he serves as President of the TPUSA Broome Tioga Activism Hub and hosts the Caleb Grubb Show.

Ryan McCombs
Columnist

Ryan is the Vice President and Former Secretary of the St. Bonaventure College Republicans. A junior Political Science major with an English minor, Ryan is an aspiring public defender and vocal member of the Conservative Movement. His work at Right Side Talks focuses on advocating for criminal justice reform and a strong national defense.

Gavin Krauciunas
Columnist

Gavin is from Upstate New York, right outside the Adirondack Park. He graduated from the University at Buffalo in December 2025 with a degree in Criminology and Law, and has strong aspirations of attending law school and becoming an attorney. A lifelong active member of the Republican Party, Gavin has worked closely with a number of public officials and looks forward to contributing to policy and public service efforts in the future.

John DePerno
Columnist

John is from Utica, New York and attends Siena College, where he majors in Political Science and minors in History. A lifelong Republican, he has worked with all three levels of government, state, local, and federal, and plans to attend law school after graduation. He looks forward to continuing his involvement in the party and contributing to the conservative movement.
